20.Trans Andean Cattleheart

Trans Andean Cattleheart (Parides iphidamas) is a native species of Central and South America. They live in warm areas in the Andes region, such as the lowlands and forests in Peru, although they are also found in Costa Rica and Mexico. Their wingspans are about 3.9 inches (10 cm).
